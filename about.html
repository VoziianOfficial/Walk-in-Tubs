About us

A homeowner-first platform built to simplify the search for walk-in tub providers.

We focus on helping people compare local companies and safer bathing solutions with more clarity, not on directly performing the work.

Why this platform exists

Choosing a walk-in tub or accessibility-focused bathroom upgrade often happens at an important life moment. Homeowners may be planning ahead for aging in place, responding to mobility changes, or helping a family member stay safer at home. That search can feel fragmented and overwhelming.

Walk-In Tub Guide was created to make that process easier. Our platform helps users review service categories, understand common project paths, and connect with local providers more efficiently.

Clarity first

We present the market in a way that is easier to understand, from installation and replacement to repair and broader accessibility upgrades.

Aggregator positioning

We are not contractors and we do not directly install, repair, or replace walk-in tubs. Our role is to help homeowners compare local companies and move forward with more confidence.

Safer choices

By simplifying comparison across many providers, we help homeowners take a more informed approach to safety, comfort, convenience, and accessibility.

How we help

We simplify search, comparison, and next steps.

Homeowners come to our platform to understand their options and request information from local providers that fit the type of project they are considering. Whether the need is a walk-in tub installation, a repair, a hydrotherapy model, or a broader bathroom accessibility upgrade, we help make the path forward easier to navigate.

We do not represent a single contractor or send an in-house service team. Instead, we help users explore suitable local companies faster and more safely.

For USA homeowners

Designed around practical decisions, not sales pressure.

Our content is written to support real homeowner questions around comfort, access, timelines, and budget expectations. The goal is a cleaner, more trustworthy way to compare providers in your area.
