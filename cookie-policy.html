What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember preferences, support functionality, and measure usage.

How We Use Cookies

Bathing Pro may use cookies for core site operation, consent preference storage, performance monitoring, and interface improvements.

Cookie Categories

We may use strictly necessary cookies for navigation and security, plus optional analytics cookies for aggregated usage insights, based on consent where required.

Your Consent Choices

On first visit, a cookie notice asks you to accept or decline optional cookies. The notice remains visible until one option is selected. You may later adjust preferences by clearing browser storage.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to delete or block cookies. Limiting cookies may affect certain site features, including preference memory and personalized interactions.

Third-Party Technologies

Some site components may rely on third-party tools for mapping, analytics, or embedded functionality. Those providers may place their own cookies based on how their services are configured.

Where required, optional technologies are only activated after a valid user consent choice.

Browser Controls and Do Not Track

You can manage many cookie settings directly in your browser preferences. Browser-level controls may override site-level cookie settings depending on device configuration.

Some browsers provide "Do Not Track" options; however, a universal response standard is not consistently supported across all services.
